Our Text Summarizer

What is it?

This is a program made using Wolfram Mathmatica that takes a big piece of text that you put into it, and summarizes it by taking the key points of the article and using them. This project was made for the HackMHS tournament, where kids who do or don't know how to code get together and try to make something from scratch. This program was our entry to the tournament.
